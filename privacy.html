Our chatbot is designed to offer paid AI services and other convenience tools to users who communicate with it via WhatsApp. We are committed to protecting the privacy of our users and have implemented measures to ensure that their personal information is safeguarded. This privacy policy outlines the types of information we collect from users and how we use that information.

Information We Collect

When you interact with our chatbot, we may collect the following types of information:

  1. Personal Information: We may collect your name, phone number, and other contact information that you provide us when you sign up for our services or subscribe to our newsletter.
  2. Conversational Data: We may collect information about your interactions with our chatbot, including the messages you send and receive, the time and date of those messages, and any other data related to your use of our services.
  3. Payment Information: If you choose to purchase our services, we do not collect payment information, such as your credit card number or other payment details.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect from users to provide our services and improve the user experience. Specifically, we may use your information for the following purposes:

  1. To provide our services and respond to your requests.
  2. To process payments for our services.
  3. To communicate with you about our services, updates, and other relevant information.
  4. To personalize your experience and improve our services.
  5. To analyze user behavior and improve our chatbot's functionality.

Data Security

We take data security seriously and have implemented measures to protect the personal information we collect from users. We use industry-standard encryption to secure data transmissions and follow best practices to safeguard user data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or destruction.

Third-Party Providers

We may use third-party providers to help us deliver our services, process payments, and analyze user behavior. These providers may have access to your personal information, but only to the extent necessary to provide their services. We ensure that all third-party providers we work with are compliant with data protection regulations and follow industry-standard security practices.

Updates to Our Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our services, industry regulations, or other relevant factors. We recommend that users review this policy periodically to stay informed of any changes.
